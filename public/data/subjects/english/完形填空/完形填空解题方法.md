# 完形填空解题方法

## 一、题型特点

完形填空是在一篇200-300词的短文中留出10-15个空白，要求考生从所给选项中选出最佳答案，使短文意思通顺、结构完整。它考查的是：
- 词汇辨析
- 语法运用
- 语篇理解
- 逻辑推理

## 二、解题步骤

### Step 1: 快速浏览全文
不要急于选择，先通读全文，了解文章大意和主旨。

### Step 2: 寻找信息线索
根据上下文语境、词语搭配、逻辑关系等线索进行选择。

### Step 3: 逐题攻克
对于有把握的题目先做，不确定的留到最后。

### Step 4: 复读检查
完成所有题目后，再读一遍，确保上下文通顺。

## 三、解题技巧

### 1. 上下文语境法
根据文章的前后逻辑关系选择答案。

**示例：**
The boy is very tall for his age. He ___ 1.8 meters already.
A. is
B. has
C. measures
D. grows

**解析：** 答案C。"measure 1.8 meters"表示"身高1.8米"。

### 2. 词语搭配法
根据常见固定搭配选择答案。

**示例：**
I'm looking forward to ___ from you soon.
A. hear
B. hearing
C. heard
D. have heard

**解析：** 答案B。"look forward to doing"是固定搭配，to是介词。

### 3. 逻辑推理法
根据文章的逻辑关系（因果、转折、递进等）选择。

**示例：**
She is very tired, ___ she still keeps studying.
A. but
B. and
C. so
D. or

**解析：** 答案A。前后是转折关系，"虽然累但仍坚持学习"。

### 4. 文化背景法
根据英语国家的文化习惯和常识判断。

**示例：**
It's not my fault. You should ___ an apology.
A. ask
B. demand
C. offer
D. take

**解析：** 答案C。"offer an apology"表示"主动道歉"，符合语境。

### 5. 语法判断法
根据语法规则判断词性和时态。

**示例：**
If it ___ tomorrow, we will stay at home.
A. rain
B. rains
C. rained
D. raining

**解析：** 答案B。主将从现原则，if条件句用一般现在时。

## 四、常考知识点

### 1. 动词相关
- 及物动词/不及物动词
- 动词短语
- 非谓语动词（to do / doing / done）
- 动词时态和语态

### 2. 名词相关
- 名词单复数
- 名词所有格
- 名词固定搭配

### 3. 形容词/副词相关
- 形容词修饰名词
- 副词修饰动词/形容词
- 形容词比较级和最高级

### 4. 连词相关
- 并列连词（and, but, or, so）
- 从属连词（because, although, if）
- 关系词（which, that, who）

### 5. 介词相关
- 地点介词（in, on, at）
- 时间介词（in, on, at）
- 动词短语中的介词

## 五、答题口诀

1. **通读全文，把握主旨**
2. **先易后难，逐一攻克**
3. **上下文找线索**
4. **固定搭配要牢记**
5. **逻辑关系是关键**
6. **代入检查保正确**

## 六、真题示例

**题型：** 完形填空
**难度：** 中等
**字数：** 约250词

Life is not easy, but I have learned to ___1___ with it.

When I was young, I always ___2___ everything my parents did for me. I never thought about the ___3___ they made. As I grew older, I began to understand their hard work.

Last year, my father lost his job. The whole family was in ___4___ . I realized that I needed to do something to help. ___5___ I was only a middle school student, I couldn't get a real job. But I could do some small things at home to reduce their ___6___ .

I started to help with the housework. I cleaned the rooms, washed the dishes, and learned to ___7___ meals. At first, I was not good at cooking. The food was sometimes too salty or too ___8___ . But I kept practicing and improved ___9___ .

Now I can cook many delicious dishes. My parents are ___10___ of me. Though life is still not easy, our family has become ___11___ than before.

I have learned that life is not about complaining. It's about facing problems and ___12___ solutions. Every small step counts.

1. A. talk B. deal C. meet D. argue
2. A. liked B. wanted C. depended on D. complained about
3. A. decisions B. efforts C. mistakes D. dreams
4. A. danger B. trouble C. silence D. peace
5. A. Because B. Though C. If D. When
6. A. pressure B. interest C. power D. knowledge
7. A. cook B. design C. serve D. taste
8. A. sweet B. sour C.淡 D. spicy
9. A. slowly B. quickly C. carefully D. happily
10. A. tired B. careful C. proud D. afraid
11. A. happier B. richer C. lazier D. weaker
12. A. finding B. forgetting C. hiding D. giving up

**答案：** 1-12: B C B B B A A C B C A A
