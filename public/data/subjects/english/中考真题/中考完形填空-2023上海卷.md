# 中考英语完形填空 - 2023年上海卷

## 题目

**Part 2 Reading Comprehension (第二部分 阅读理解)**

**Section C (C节) Cloze Test (完形填空)**

Directions: Fill in the blanks with proper words. Each blank in the following passage should be filled with only ONE word.

Last summer, I went to Beijing for my summer camp. It was my first time to visit the capital city of China. I was very __1__ because I had never been so far away from home before.

On the first morning, we __2__ up early and visited the Great Wall. When I stood on the wall and looked around, I was amazed by its __3__. "It's so long and beautiful!" I said to myself. The tour guide told us that the Great Wall was built more than two thousand years __4__. It was really a great wonder in the world.

In the afternoon, we went to the Palace Museum. There were many __5__ buildings and old things inside. I learned a lot about Chinese history there. The people who worked there were very __6__ and they answered all our questions patiently.

On the third day, we visited the Bird's Nest, the Olympic stadium. I took many __7__ there. I also bought some souvenirs for my parents and friends. I felt __8__ but happy during the trip.

This trip was really __9__. I not only learned more about Chinese history and culture but also made new friends. I will never __10__ this wonderful experience.

1. A. excited B. sad C. bored D. tired
2. A. got B. woke C. stood D. went
3. A. size B. beauty C. age D. shape
4. A. before B. ago C. later D. after
5. A. modern B. tall C. beautiful D. big
6. A. busy B. friendly C. serious D. strict
7. A. notes B. photos C. books D. drawings
8. A. lonely B. homesick C. happy D. busy
9. A. terrible B. boring C. amazing D. useless
10. A. forget B. remember C. keep D. lose

---

## 答案解析

1. **答案：A**
   解析：根据后文"had never been so far away from home"可知是兴奋/紧张的心情。

2. **答案：A**
   解析：get up是固定搭配，意为"起床"。

3. **答案：B**
   解析：根据前文"amazed"和后文"so long and beautiful"可知是赞美其美丽。

4. **答案：B**
   解析：two thousand years ago表示两千年前。

5. **答案：C**
   解析：beautiful buildings与全文对故宫的赞美语气一致。

6. **答案：B**
   解析：friendly表示友好，与"answered all our questions patiently"一致。

7. **答案：B**
   解析：take photos是固定搭配，意为"拍照"。

8. **答案：B**
   解析：homesick表示想家的，与第一次离家远行相呼应。

9. **答案：C**
   解析：根据全文内容可知这是一次令人惊叹的旅行。

10. **答案：A**
    解析：never forget表示永远不会忘记，是对此经历的总结。
