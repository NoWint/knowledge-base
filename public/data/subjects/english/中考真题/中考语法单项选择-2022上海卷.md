# 中考英语语法单项选择 - 2022年上海卷

## 题目

**Part 1 Vocabulary and Grammar (第一部分 词汇和语法)**

**Section A (A节) Choose the best answer (选择最恰当的答案)**

1. My father is ___ engineer. He works in a car factory.
   A. a B. an C. the D. /

2. The weather in Shanghai is hotter than ___ in Beijing.
   A. it B. that C. this D. those

3. The children ___ the art museum last Friday.
   A. visit B. visited C. will visit D. are visiting

4. I have ___ my best friend since we were in primary school.
   A. known B. know C. will know D. knew

5. ___ important it is to protect the environment!
   A. What B. What an C. How D. How an

6. He asked me where I ___ .
   A. live B. lived C. will live D. living

7. The book is worth ___ many times.
   A. read B. reading C. to read D. reads

8. Not only ___ the students but also the teacher ___ the film.
   A. like; likes B. likes; like C. like; like D. likes; likes

9. If it ___ tomorrow, we will stay at home.
   A. rains B. rained C. will rain D. rain

10. The news ___ very interesting.
    A. are B. is C. were D. be

---

## 答案解析

1. **答案：B**
   解析：engineer以元音音素开头，用an修饰。

2. **答案：B**
   解析：that指代前文提到的the weather，避免重复。

3. **答案：B**
   解析：last Friday表示过去时间，用一般过去时。

4. **答案：A**
   解析：since引导的时间状语从句，主句用现在完成时。

5. **答案：C**
   解析：How + adj. + 主语 + 谓语！是感叹句结构。

6. **答案：B**
   解析：asked是过去式，宾语从句用相应的过去时态。

7. **答案：B**
   解析：be worth doing sth. 表示值得做某事。

8. **答案：A**
   解析：Not only...but also遵循就近原则，the teacher是单数，所以用likes。

9. **答案：A**
   解析：条件状语从句用一般现在时表将来。

10. **答案：B**
    解析：news是不可数名词，谓语用单数。
