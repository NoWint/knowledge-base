# 中考英语写作范文 - 我的朋友

## 题目要求

请你根据以下提示，写一篇介绍你最好朋友的短文。内容包括：
1. 你们是怎么认识的
2. 他/她的外貌和性格特点
3. 你们经常一起做什么
4. 为什么你们能成为好朋友

词数：80-100词

---

## 优秀范文

**My Best Friend**

I have many friends, but my best friend is Li Ming. We have known each other since we started primary school eight years ago.

Li Ming is a boy of medium height with short black hair and bright eyes. What impresses me most is his friendly smile. He is always ready to help others and never says a bad word about anyone.

We often study together after school. We share our problems and joys with each other. On weekends, we sometimes play basketball or go cycling.

I think we can become good friends because we have similar interests and we trust each other. A true friend reaches for your hand and touches your heart. I am lucky to have such a best friend.

（共98词）

---

## 范文解析

### 结构分析

| 部分 | 内容 | 句型 |
|------|------|------|
| 第一段 | 引言：介绍朋友身份和认识时间 | My best friend is... We have known each other since... |
| 第二段 | 外貌和性格特点 | ...is a boy of medium height with... What impresses me most is... |
| 第三段 | 共同活动 | We often... We share... On weekends, we sometimes... |
| 第四段 | 总结原因和感悟 | I think we can become good friends because... A true friend... |

### 亮点句型

1. **外貌描写**：a boy of medium height with short black hair and bright eyes
2. **特点强调**：What impresses me most is his friendly smile.
3. **常用表达**：be ready to help others / say a bad word about anyone
4. **情感升华**：A true friend reaches for your hand and touches your heart.

### 核心词汇

| 单词 | 释义 |
|------|------|
| medium height | 中等身高 |
| bright eyes | 明亮的眼睛 |
| friendly smile | 友好的微笑 |
| be ready to | 愿意... |
| similar interests | 相似的兴趣 |
| trust | v. 信任 |
| true friend | 真正的朋友 |
