# 中考英语写作范文 - 一次旅行

## 题目要求

请你根据以下提示，以"A Trip to Beijing"为题，写一篇短文。

内容包括：
1. 旅行的时间、同行人员和目的地
2. 参观了几个地方，看到了什么
3. 你最喜欢的景点是哪个，为什么
4. 这次旅行的感受

词数：80-100词

---

## 优秀范文

**A Trip to Beijing**

Last summer holiday, my family and I went to Beijing for a trip. It was my first visit to the capital city of China.

On the first day, we visited the Great Wall. I was amazed by its grand scale and beautiful scenery. "What a wonderful view!" I couldn't help saying. The next day, we went to the Palace Museum, where we learned a lot about Chinese history. In the evening, we enjoyed delicious Beijing duck.

Among all the places, I like the Great Wall best. It is not only a wonder of the world but also a symbol of the Chinese nation. This trip was really amazing. I will never forget it.

（共89词）

---

## 范文解析

### 结构分析

| 部分 | 内容 | 句型 |
|------|------|------|
| 第一段 | 旅行基本信息 | ...went to Beijing for a trip. It was my first visit to... |
| 第二段 | 具体行程安排 | On the first day... The next day... In the evening... |
| 第三段 | 最喜欢的景点及理由 | Among all the places, I like... best. It is not only... but also... |
| 第四段 | 总结感受 | This trip was really amazing. I will never forget it. |

### 亮点句型

1. **旅行开头**：my family and I went to Beijing for a trip
2. **感叹句**：What a wonderful view! / I couldn't help saying
3. **定语从句**：the Palace Museum, where we learned a lot about Chinese history
4. **最高级**：Among all the places, I like... best
5. **并列结构**：not only... but also...

| 单词 | 释义 |
