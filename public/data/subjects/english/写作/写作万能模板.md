# 英语写作万能模板

## 一、书信类

### 1. 建议信
**开头：**
- I am glad to receive your letter.
- It's a pleasure for me to hear that...

**正文：**
Here are some suggestions / pieces of advice for you:
- First of all, you are advised to...
- In addition, it would be helpful if you could...
- Last but not least,...

**结尾：**
I hope these suggestions will be helpful to you.
If you have any further questions, please don't hesitate to contact me.
I am looking forward to your early reply.

### 2. 道歉信
**开头：**
I am writing to apologize for...
I am really sorry for...

**正文：**
The main reason is that...
I feel terribly sorry about it.

**结尾：**
I hope you can accept my sincere apology.
I will try my best not to make the same mistake again.
Once again, I am sorry for any inconvenience caused.

### 3. 感谢信
**开头：**
I am writing to express my thanks/gratitude to you.
I would like to thank you from the bottom of my heart for...

**正文：**
Your help/support made it possible for me to...
Without your help, I couldn't have succeeded in...

**结尾：**
Please accept my sincere gratitude.
I will never forget your kindness.
I am looking forward to your reply.

## 二、议论文

### 1. 观点对比类
**引言段：**
- Recently, there has been a heated discussion about whether... / ...has become a hot topic.
- Different people hold different views.

**正文段：**
On the one hand, some people support that...
They argue that...
On the other hand, others believe that...
In their view,...

**结论段：**
In my opinion / As for me, I prefer... / I am in favor of...
Because...
Therefore, I think...

### 2. 问题解决类
**引言段：**
It is reported that... / Recently, ... has become a serious problem.
As we all know, ...

**正文段：**
There are several ways to solve this problem.
First of all, we should... / The first step is to...
In addition, it is necessary to...
Finally,...

**结论段：**
Only in this way can we solve the problem.
If everyone can do something, our environment will be better and better.

## 三、记叙文

### 1. 人物介绍
I would like to introduce my best friend/teacher...
He/She is ... years old.
He/She has long black hair and big eyes.
What impresses me most is that he/she is always ready to help others.
He/She is not only hardworking but also kind-hearted.
I am lucky to have such a good friend/teacher.

### 2. 难忘的经历
I still remember the day when...
It happened on a sunny morning / a rainy afternoon.
At that time, I was...
Suddenly, I realized that...
From this experience, I learned that...

### 3. 旅行经历
Last summer holiday, I went to... with my family.
It is located in the eastern/southern/northern part of China.
The trip was really enjoyable/ unforgettable.
The most exciting part was...
I will never forget this wonderful trip.

## 四、应用文

### 1. 通知
NOTICE
The Student Union will organize a ... activity.
All the students are welcome to take part in.
Time: ... (Date and Time)
Place: ... (Location)
Please come on time.
Contact: ... (Phone number or person)

### 2. 海报
POSTER
Welcome to our ...
An exciting ... will be held by ...
Time: ...
Place: ...
Don't miss the chance!
Come and join us!
For more information, please contact...

### 3. 发言稿
Good morning/afternoon, everyone!
It's my honor to speak here.
Today I would like to talk about...
In my opinion,...
That's all. Thank you for listening.

## 五、常用句型

### 开头句
- Recently, ... has become a hot topic.
- It is well-known that...
- As we all know, ...
- There is no doubt that...

### 表观点
- In my opinion / As for me / Personally, I...
- I strongly believe that...
- I agree with the opinion that...

### 表原因
- The main reason is that...
- This is because...
- Due to / Because of / Thanks to...

### 表结果
- As a result / Therefore / Thus / So...
- It leads to / results in...
- This made it possible for us to...

### 表总结
- In conclusion / In short / In a word...
- To sum up / All in all...
- From what has been discussed above...

### 表期望
- I hope that...
- I am looking forward to...
- I sincerely hope that...

## 六、常用连接词

### 时间顺序
- first of all / firstly / first
- then / next / after that
- finally / last but not least

### 因果关系
- because / since / as
- so / therefore / as a result
- because of / due to / owing to

### 转折关系
- but / however / nevertheless
- although / though / even though
- while / on the contrary

### 并列关系
- and / also / as well as
- both...and... / neither...nor...
- not only...but also...

### 递进关系
- what's more / moreover / besides
- in addition / additionally
- to make matters worse / worse still
