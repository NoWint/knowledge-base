# 中考英语写作范文 - 保护环境

## 题目要求

请你根据以下提示，以"How to Protect the Environment"为题，写一篇短文。

内容包括：
1. 为什么要保护环境
2. 我们可以采取哪些措施保护环境
3. 号召大家一起行动

词数：80-100词

---

## 优秀范文

**How to Protect the Environment**

It is well-known that the environment is becoming worse and worse. We must take action to protect it before it's too late.

There are many ways we can help. First of all, we should plant more trees and flowers to make our city greener and more beautiful. In addition, we should reduce the use of plastic bags and recycle more waste things. What's more, it's important for us to save energy by turning off lights and taps when we don't use them. Finally, we should raise people's awareness of environmental protection.

As students, we can start with small things around us. Only by working together can we make the world a better place to live in.

（共92词）

---

## 范文解析

### 结构分析

| 部分 | 内容 | 句型 |
|------|------|------|
| 第一段 | 引言：问题背景 | It is well-known that... We must take action... |
| 第二段 | 主体：具体措施 | First of all... In addition... What's more... Finally... |
| 第三段 | 结尾：号召行动 | As students... Only by working together can we... |

### 亮点句型

1. **问题引出**：It is well-known that... / ...is becoming worse and worse
2. **措施列举**：
   - First of all / In addition / What's more / Finally
   - ...to make our city greener and more beautiful
3. **重要性**：it's important for us to do something
4. **倒装句**：Only by working together can we make the world a better place to live in.

### 核心词汇

| 单词 | 释义 |
|------|------|
| environment | n. 环境 |
| protect | v. 保护 |
| take action | 采取行动 |
| plant | v. 种植 |
| reduce | v. 减少 |
| recycle | v. 回收利用 |
| save energy | 节约能源 |
| raise awareness | 提高意识 |
| environmental protection | 环境保护 |
