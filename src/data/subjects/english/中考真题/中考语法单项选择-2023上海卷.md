# 中考英语语法单项选择 - 2023年上海卷

## 题目

**Part 1 Vocabulary and Grammar (第一部分 词汇和语法)**

**Section A (A节) Choose the best answer (选择最恰当的答案)**

1. The book on the desk is very interesting. "on the desk" is a phrase.
   A. attributive B. adverbial C. complement D. predicate

2. He went to the school library yesterday and borrowed two books.
   A. a; the B. the; / C. /; two D. the; two

3. The little girl is too young to ___ herself.
   A. wear B. dress C. put on D. have on

4. The film ___ for ten minutes when I got to the cinema.
   A. had been on B. had begun C. has begun D. began

5. It is necessary for us ___ English well in the modern world.
   A. learning B. to learn C. learn D. learned

6. The teacher asked the students ___ a composition about their dreams.
   A. write B. writing C. to write D. wrote

7. She has been to Beijing twice ___ she came to Shanghai.
   A. when B. before C. since D. after

8. ___ lovely baby she is!
   A. What a B. What C. How D. How a

9. I don't think this story is as interesting as that one, ___ ?
   A. do I B. don't I C. is it D. isn't it

10. The old man lived ___ , but he never felt ___ .
   A. alone; alone B. lonely; lonely C. alone; lonely D. lonely; alone

---

## 答案解析

1. **答案：B**
   解析："on the desk"在句中作地点状语，修饰动词went。

2. **答案：D**
   解析：go to the school表示"去学校"，school前用the；borrow是及物动词，直接跟宾语books。

3. **答案：B**
   解析：dress oneself表示"自己穿衣服"，dress可以反身代词作宾语。wear和put on表示穿的动作，不跟反身代词。

4. **答案：A**
   解析：when引导的状语从句用一般过去时，主句用过去完成时。"电影已开始十分钟"用had been on（延续性动词）。

5. **答案：B**
   解析：It is + adj. + for sb. + to do sth. 是固定句型。

6. **答案：C**
   解析：ask sb. to do sth. 要求某人做某事。

7. **答案：C**
   解析：since表示"自从"，常与现在完成时连用。

8. **答案：A**
   解析：感叹句结构：What + a/an + adj. + n. + 主语 + 谓语！

9. **答案：C**
   解析：否定转移。I don't think...是否定前移，反意疑问句与从句一致，用is it。

10. **答案：C**
    解析：alone表示"独自一人"，客观状态；lonely表示"孤独的"，主观感受。
