# 中考英语写作范文 - 我的梦想

## 题目要求

请你根据以下提示，以"My Dream"为题，写一篇短文。

内容包括：
1. 你的梦想是什么
2. 为什么会有这个梦想
3. 你打算如何实现梦想
4. 实现梦想后的打算

词数：80-100词

---

## 优秀范文

**My Dream**

Everyone has a dream, and so do I. My dream is to become a doctor in the future.

The reason why I want to be a doctor is quite personal. When I was a child, my grandmother was seriously ill. The doctors in the local hospital saved her life with their professional skills and careful treatment. From then on, I decided to become a doctor to help more people like my grandmother.

To achieve my dream, I must study hard and try my best to get good grades. Besides, I will exercise more to keep healthy. Most importantly, I will never give up my dream no matter how difficult it is.

If my dream comes true, I will serve the people heart and soul in a hospital.

（共105词）

---

## 范文解析

### 结构分析

| 部分 | 内容 | 句型 |
|------|------|------|
| 第一段 | 点明梦想 | Everyone has a dream, and so do I. My dream is to... |
| 第二段 | 原因说明 | The reason why... is quite personal. When I was... From then on... |
| 第三段 | 实现计划 | To achieve my dream, I must... Besides... Most importantly... |
| 第四段 | 未来打算 | If my dream comes true, I will... |

### 亮点句型

1. **梦想表达**：Everyone has a dream, and so do I. / My dream is to become...
2. **原因说明**：The reason why I want to be... is quite personal.
3. **时间回顾**：When I was a child... From then on, I decided to...
4. **决心表达**：no matter how difficult it is / never give up
5. **条件句**：If my dream comes true, I will...

### 核心词汇

| 单词 | 释义 |
|------|------|
| dream | n. 梦想 |
| doctor | n. 医生 |
| seriously ill | 病重 |
| professional skills | 专业技能 |
| achieve | v. 实现 |
| exercise | v. 锻炼 |
| give up | 放弃 |
| come true | 实现 |
| serve | v. 服务 |
