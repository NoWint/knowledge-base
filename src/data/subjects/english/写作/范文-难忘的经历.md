# 中考英语写作范文 - 难忘的经历

## 题目要求

请根据以下提示，以"An Unforgettable Experience"为题，写一篇短文。

内容包括：
1. 这件事发生的时间、地点和原因
2. 事情的经过
3. 你的感受和从中得到的启示

词数：80-100词

---

## 优秀范文

**An Unforgettable Experience**

Last summer, I had an unforgettable experience that changed my life.

One morning, I was walking home from the library when it suddenly began to rain heavily. I had no umbrella with me, so I ran to a nearby shop to take shelter. To my surprise, an old lady offered me a cup of hot tea and a warm smile. She said, "Young man, don't worry. The rain will stop soon."

The rain did stop after an hour. Before I left, I thanked her from the bottom of my heart. This experience taught me that kindness can warm both others and ourselves. A small act of kindness can make a big difference in someone's day.

Since then, I have tried my best to help others in need.

（共103词）

---

## 范文解析

### 结构分析

| 部分 | 内容 | 句型 |
|------|------|------|
| 第一段 | 引言：点明主题 | Last summer, I had an unforgettable experience that... |
| 第二段 | 经过：描述事件发生过程 | One morning, I was walking home when... To my surprise... |
| 第三段 | 感悟：从经历中得到的启示 | This experience taught me that... A small act of kindness can... |
| 第四段 | 行动：之后的改变 | Since then, I have tried my best to... |

### 亮点句型

1. **时间句型**：One morning/morning/afternoon, I was doing something when...
2. **意外发现**：To my surprise, ... / Imagine my surprise when...
3. **感谢表达**：thank sb. from the bottom of my heart
4. **感悟总结**：This experience taught me that...
5. **行动改变**：Since then, I have tried my best to...

### 核心词汇

| 单词 | 释义 |
|------|------|
| unforgettable | adj. 难忘的 |
| take shelter | 躲避 |
| to my surprise | 令我惊讶的是 |
| kindness | n. 善良 |
| warmth | v. 使...温暖 |
| make a difference | 产生影响 |
| in need | 处于困境中 |
| since then | 从那以后 |
